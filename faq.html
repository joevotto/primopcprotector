Frequently Asked Questions

Will future support requests be free?
Within 2 weeks of the original support session, if the same issue occurs, next support session will be free. Note that if a new issue is in need of support, that will not be a free session.
Discounts for students and Non Profit Organizations?
Unfortunately, I am unable to offer any discounts or promotions at this time.
Are you able to come to my house/office, or send someone out?
I am the only person doing the support, so all of my support will be done via email or chat and I do offer remote access so I can remote into a computer to assist that way.
Any refunds?
I am unable to offer refunds, but if the issue is not fixed then within the following 2 weeks you will get a free session to fix that issue.
What Remote Software Do You Use?
I use Teamviewer.
How do I contact you for support?
You can use the chat function on the Contact Me Page, you can send me an email using the form on the Contact Me page, or purchase services using the tillypay link on the billing page.
